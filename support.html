Frequently Asked Questions

How does CaricatureMe work?

Take a selfie (or choose a photo), pick a scene and personality tags, and our AI generates a hilarious cartoon caricature of you in that scene. It takes just seconds!

Is my photo stored anywhere?

No. Your photo is sent to our AI provider for real-time generation only. We do not store your photos on any server. Generated caricatures are saved locally on your device.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad. You can change or cancel your subscription at any time.

Can I get a refund?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

Why doesn't the caricature look exactly like me?

Caricatures are exaggerated cartoons — that's the fun! The AI preserves your recognizable features while adding comedic exaggeration. For best results, use a well-lit, front-facing photo.

Can I delete my caricatures?

Yes! Tap any caricature in your Gallery and select Delete. You can also delete all data by removing the app from your device.
